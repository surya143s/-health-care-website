Our Departments

Comprehensive care under one roof — excellence across every specialty.

Specialized Medical Excellence

LifeCare Health Clinic is home to an extensive network of specialized departments designed to offer advanced, patient-focused healthcare. From cardiology to radiology, each department is staffed by world-class professionals equipped with modern diagnostic tools and treatment facilities.

Our Medical Departments

Cardiology

Our Cardiology unit provides advanced cardiac care, including angioplasty, echocardiograms, and preventive heart checkups.

Radiology

State-of-the-art MRI, CT scans, ultrasound, and digital X-ray imaging for precise diagnosis and treatment planning.

Pediatrics

Comprehensive healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents, focusing on growth, wellness, and preventive care.

Orthopedics

Providing treatment for bone and joint disorders, fractures, sports injuries, and post-surgical rehabilitation.

Neurology

Diagnosis and treatment of brain, spinal cord, and nervous system disorders by highly experienced neurologists.

Gynecology

Comprehensive women’s health services, prenatal care, fertility treatments, and gynecological surgeries.

Pathology

Advanced laboratory and diagnostic testing ensuring accurate results and quick turnaround times.

Dermatology

Expert care for skin, hair, and nail conditions using modern therapies and cosmetic dermatology solutions.
